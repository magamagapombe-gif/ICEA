Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Tap any question to expand the answer.

What is Investa Plus?
+
Investa Plus is a savings and investment plan by ICEA Lion Life Assurance Uganda. You save a fixed amount every month for 5, 6, or 7 years and at the end you receive your savings plus guaranteed profits — all tax free. Your family is also protected with life cover throughout the plan.
Does it work with my bank or mobile money?
+
Yes — 100%. Investa Plus works with any bank in Uganda (Equity, Stanbic, DFCU, Centenary, Absa, KCB, PostBank and more) and any mobile money (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money).
I don't have a bank account. Can I still join?
+
Absolutely. Batema Kenneth will personally help you open an Equity Bank account for free — the same day. All you need is your National ID. Mobile money (MTN or Airtel) also works without a bank account.
Isn't 5 or 7 years too long to wait?
+
Think about where you were 5 years ago. That time passed quickly, didn't it? 5 years is just 60 monthly payments. The time will pass either way. The question is: will you have something to show for it?
What is the minimum I can save monthly?
+
The minimum monthly premium is UGX 200,000. There is no maximum — the more you save, the bigger your payout at maturity.
What happens if I miss a monthly payment?
+
ICEA Lion has a paid-up provision. If you face financial challenges, your plan doesn't collapse — it becomes "paid up" and you still receive the value of what you've already saved, plus proportional profits, at maturity.
Is my money safe with ICEA Lion?
+
Absolutely. ICEA Lion has been operating in East Africa since 1964 — over 60 years. They are licensed and regulated by the Insurance Regulatory Authority (IRA) of Uganda. Your investment is fully protected under Ugandan law.
What does "Sum Assured" mean?
+
Sum Assured is the total amount you are guaranteed to receive at the end of your plan term. It includes all your monthly savings plus the guaranteed profit added on top.
How do I start? How long does it take?
+
Send Batema a WhatsApp message or call him. He'll guide you through the documents, fill in the forms with you, and your plan can be active within 1–2 working days. Batema can come to you.
Are the returns really tax free?
+
Yes — 100% tax free. All profits earned from your Investa Plus plan are exempt from income tax under Ugandan insurance regulations. There are also no monthly management fees or hidden charges.
Can I get a loan against my policy?
+
Yes. After your policy has been active for a period of time and has built sufficient value, you may be eligible to borrow against the cash value of your policy. Contact Batema for specific eligibility criteria.
